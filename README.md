RoomieShare Quick Start Guide

1. Account Setup
Sign Up / Login: Open the app, register with your email, password, and username, or log in if you already have an account.
Edit Profile: Tap your profile avatar on the Home screen to update your display name at any time.

2. Roommate Group Setup
Go to the Group tab to connect with your roommates:
Create Group: Type a group name to generate a unique 8-character invitation code. As creator (👑), you can rename the group or remove members.
Join Group: Paste the code shared by your roommates to instantly sync database lists.

3. Dashboard (Home)
View your daily welcome message and current date.
Track your pending tasks with the Chore Progress Bar (e.g., "2 of 5 done").
Use the Monthly Calendar to see your chore deadlines highlighted with dot indicators.

4. Chores
Create: Tap the + button, enter a title/description, and pick a due date.
Auto-Assign: The app automatically rotates chores among roommates (round-robin) to keep the workload fair.
Track: Mark off chores under Your Tasks or view active chores in Members' Tasks.

5. Groceries
Add & Shop: Fast-add items to the list. Check them off to move them from To Buy to Purchased.
Auto-Sync: Grocery expenses logged in the Bills tab automatically sync to this list (e.g., "Milk: ₱150.00").

6. Expenses & Bills
Log Expenses: Add costs under Bills, Grocery, or Others.
Split Bills: The app automatically divides the total costs by the number of roommates to display your exact share.
Settle Up: Check the box next to any bill to mark it Paid (grays it out with a strikethrough).
